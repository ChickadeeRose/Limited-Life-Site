The Bad Boys

Ⲻ Ⲻ Ⲻ
The Bad Boys were a group in Limited Life consisting of Grian, SolidarityGaming (AKA Jimmy), and SmallishBeans (AKA Joel).
This alliance was made during the first session by Joel and Jimmy teaming up after Joel trampled another team's crops and didn't replant them claiming to be the Bad Boy of the server.
The two decided to base on top of the Woodland Mansion that was in the middle of the map, and recruited Grian to the Bad Boys after finding that he had beat them to the mansion. After Jimmy and Joel went out, Grian went and joined the Nosy Neighbors until he died.
  1. Jimmy went out of the series during Session 7 due to fall damage, ending in 14th place. (haha last L)
  2. Joel went out of the series also during Session 7 due to being killed by Smajor1995 (AKA Scott), ending in 12th place.
  3. Grian went out of the series during Session 8 due to fall damage, ending in 6th place.
